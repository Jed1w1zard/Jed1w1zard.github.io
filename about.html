About Me

My name is Nathaniel Nichols. I am a student at the University of Rhode Island with a double major in Philosophy and German, with the intent to add a major in Computer Science and a minor in Art. I plan to participate in URI's ICSP (International Computer Science Program). I work as a math tutor at URI and as the Bass section leader of the Rhode Island Children's Chorus, and I serve as a staff member of the Metropolis of Boston Camp in the summer. I'm an avid fan of sci-fi and fantasy books and movies, especially Star Wars, Dr. Who, Harry Potter, and The Lord of the Rings. In my free time, I enjoy drawing, painting, reading, writing, singing, playing guitar, walking, cycling, and taking care of the family dogs and chickens.
